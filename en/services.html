Legal services in migration and documentation

We handle every case with the same dedication: the legal rigor of a law firm with the closeness of a personal advisor.

Block 01

International visas

Legal advisory and processing of visa applications before consulates and immigration authorities across multiple jurisdictions, in accordance with current regulations.

  • Tourist / short-stay visa
  • Business visa
  • Work permit visa
  • Student visa
  • Temporary residence visa
  • Permanent residence visa
  • Family reunification
  • Investor / digital nomad visa
  • Renewals and status changes
  • Apostille and legalization
  • Appeals and reconsiderations
  • Consular interview coaching
Visa type Estimated timeline Complexity
Tourist / short-stay 10–20 business days Low
Student 4–8 weeks Medium
Work 8–16 weeks High
Permanent residence 6–12 months High
Family reunification 6–10 months High

Timelines are indicative and depend on the consulate, the applicant's nationality and the season.

Block 02

Passports

We process issuances, renewals and special cases of ordinary and official passports, ensuring full compliance with formal and documentary requirements.

  • First issuance (adults and minors)
  • Standard renewal
  • Renewal for exhausted pages
  • Replacement after loss or theft
  • Passport for minors
  • Biographical data corrections
  • Photo or data updates
  • Apostille of supporting documents
  • Urgent or emergency processing
  • Itinerant consulate advisory
  • Coordination with consular offices
  • In-person interview accompaniment
Procedure Typical timeline Documentation
First issuance 3–6 weeks ID, photo, birth certificate
Standard renewal 2–5 weeks Previous passport
Lost / stolen replacement 3–8 weeks Police report, alternate ID
Minors 4–8 weeks Both parents + birth certificate
Urgent processing 24–72 hours Variable by consulate
