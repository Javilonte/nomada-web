The firm

Professionalism, ethics and closeness

Nómada was born from a conviction: immigration advisory requires legal precision and human sensitivity. We bring both to every case.

Our story

15 years accompanying international procedures

Founded in 2009 by attorneys trained in private international law, Nómada arose in response to the growing need for professional, bilingual legal support in immigration processes. Throughout these years we have represented individuals from more than 35 nationalities and companies across multiple sectors.

Our practice focuses exclusively on international mobility: visas, passports, residence and consular documentation. This specialization enables us to maintain current and deep knowledge of the regulations of the main jurisdictions.

We maintain a strict policy of confidentiality and regulatory compliance, and we work only with clients whose documentation and situation allows, in our professional judgment, a legitimate process.

Our values

Principles that guide our practice

Legal rigor

Every application is evaluated in accordance with current regulations and applicable case law. We do not take on cases we cannot substantiate.

Confidentiality

All information provided is handled with the strictest professional privilege.

Transparency

Clear fees, written quotes and consistent communication on every procedure's progress.

Commitment

Each file carries the personal accountability of the assigned attorney, without impersonal rotation.

Bilingualism

Professional service in English and Spanish eliminates intermediaries and reduces interpretation errors.

Ethics

We do not guarantee results. Advisory is based on real probabilities and potential risks.
